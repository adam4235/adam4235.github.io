Texting pictures to me

I can't receive pictures or other files via text; I can only receive simple texts. I made this page because people often try to text pictures to me. This is what I see when someone tries to text me a picture or other file:

The reason for this is my cell phone plan doesn't have data, and texting pictures or files are a special type of message called MMS which requires the person receiving the picture/file to use data. I don't know why I can't receive these messages over wifi, but I can't; I think it's a limitation of my cell phone provider.

Three other ways you could send me pictures instead are Email, Messenger, or Element (matrix). Of these, email seems like the simplest and best to me.
