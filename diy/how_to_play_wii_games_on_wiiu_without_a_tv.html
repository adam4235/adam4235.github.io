How To Play Wii Games On Wii U Without A TV

  1. Go to settings.
  2. Set video output to "no HDMI".
  3. Boot up the WiiU and hold B so you get directly into Wii mode. Now the Gamepad is your "TV".

This works because the Wii U gamepad has a built-in sensor bar, so you don't need the Wii sensor bar.
