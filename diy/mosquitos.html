Strategies to Avoid Mosquitos

  • Bug Jacket / Bug Hat
  • Put geranium plants, citronella candles or fresh onion slices on windowsills to ward them off
  • Remove water/wet areas
  • They're out more often during dusk and dawn
  • Wear light colored clothing
  • Wear a hat
  • Attract birds that eat mosquitos (like Purple Martins)
  • If you get a bite, then within 2 hours run hot water over a spoon (or otherwise heat up a spoon, but not too hot) then hold the spoon on the bite for 30 seconds. This reduces the itchiness.
