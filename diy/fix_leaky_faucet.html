How To Fix A Leaky Faucet

Mainly followed instructions here.

Some things to remember:

  1. Screw the large metal thing back on before turning the water back on - otherwise water spurts up the handle and knocks it off
  2. Use a rag to absorb the water in the tap before trying to get the washer and spring out - that makes it easier. Also don't push the washer and spring in further or they'll be quite hard to get out.
