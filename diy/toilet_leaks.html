How To Check If Your Toilet Is Leaking

Toilet leaks are invisible, waste water, and cost you money.

Take the toilet leak test:

  1. Open the lid of the toilet tank.
  2. Put a drop of food colouring in the toilet tank.
  3. After 10 min, check the water in the toilet bowl. If it's still clear, then there's no leak. If some of the food colouring is now in the bowl, then there's a leak.
