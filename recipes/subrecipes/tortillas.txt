3.5 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons salt
0.5 cup vegetable oil
0.75 cup hot water

-Combine dry ingredients (but only 3c flour) and mix
-Gradually mix in oil
-Gradually mix in water
-Heat a frying pan to very hot (Med-High on my stove), without oil
-Roll into 6 balls
-Roll a bit of flour on the outside of each one
-Roll out each ball very thin
-Fry each one on each side for about 10-30 seconds (flip when it starts bubbling)
