0.5 # onion
1 clove garlic
1 bunch beet greens
2 tbsp vegetable oil

-Heat up the vegetable oil in a frying pan
-Chop the onion and garlic, adding them to the frying pan
-Rip up the beet greens (I guess you leave off the stems) and add them the frying pan
-Fry until wilted
