1 package fettucini noodles
2.5 c milk
3 T white flour
3 T butter
0.5 tsp salt
1.5 grated_c cheese

-For noodles, follow package
Cheese sauce:
-Microwave butter
-Stir in flour and salt until smooth
-Whisk in milk
-Microwave 3 minutes at 600W
-Stir
-Repeat {
	-Microwave 2 minutes at 600W
	-Stir
-} until pretty thick
-Add cheese
-Microwave 2 minutes at 600W
-Stir
