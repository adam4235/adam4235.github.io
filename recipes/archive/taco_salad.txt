taco_salad
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):5
prep.:30
cook:15
recipe source: Amy (Caris recipe exchange)

1 lb ground beef 
1 package taco seasoning 
1 # head lettuce
1 # green pepper
1 # red onion
1 block cheese
1 c French dressing
1 bag corn chips

Cook beef, adding taco seasoning to beef when cooked and stir, let simmer until it thickens. 
Chop all veggies up, mix ingredients together.
