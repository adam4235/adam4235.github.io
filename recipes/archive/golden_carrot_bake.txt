golden_carrot_bake
vegetable:1
meat:0
yield (meals * people):6
prep.:15
cook:90
recipe source:Simply in Season, modified

6 # carrots
3 c water
1.33 c rice
0.5 tsp salt
0.25 tsp black pepper
3 grated_c cheese (about 1 package of fake cheese)
2 c milk
4 # eggs
0.5 # onion
1 bit cinnamon

Grate carrots
Boil everything up to and including pepper in a frying pan or pot for 45 min, or in a pressure cooker for 15 min (less if not brown rice).
In the meantime, chop onion
Preheat oven to 350F
Add other ingredients, beating eggs into the mixture
Put in baking dishes
Bake for 1 hour
