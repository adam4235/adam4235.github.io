2 lb ground beef
2 t salt
1 t black pepper
2 # onion
2 stalks celery
2 c peas
3 c noodles ( or use twice as much leftover cooked noodles)
1 grated_c cheese

-Brown beef in a skillet with salt and pepper
-While beef is browning, cook noodles according to package directions
-Chop the onion and celery
-Add them and the peas once the beef is browned
-Spread cooked noodles over meat mixture.  
-Sprinkle cheese over all
-Simmer uncovered for 15 min to blend flavours.
