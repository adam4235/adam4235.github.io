This is the nut loaf recipe before improving it by trying combinations from http://www.veganlunchbox.com/loaf_studio.html
nut_loaf
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):8
prep.:15
cook:40
recipe source:http://www.veg-world.com/recipes/nutroast.htm

2 bit oil
2 # onion
4 cloves garlic
10 # mushrooms
2 tbsp flour
2.5 c water
2 # soup cubes
3 c chopped nuts
6 slices bread
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp dried herbs
2 bit black pepper

-Chop and fry onion
-Chop and add mushrooms
-Chop and add garlic
-Sprinkle flour on top.  Stir to mix it in.
-Add stock and water, stirring constantly until it comes to a boil.
-Simmer for 2-3 min.
-Preheat oven to 375F
-Make bread into breadcrumbs
-Add them and everything else, stirring well.
-Grease a baking dish.
-Sprinkle flour in the baking dish, then put in the mixture, then sprinkle flour on it.
-Bake for 30-40 min.
