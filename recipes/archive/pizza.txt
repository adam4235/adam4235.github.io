pizza
vegetable: 1
meat: 1
yield (meals * people): 2
prep.: 5
cook: 20

1 # pizza crust
1 bunch tomato sauce
1 bunch cheese
1 bunch pepperoni
1 bunch onions

chop the onion.
grate the cheese.
cut the pepperoni.
Put it all together.
Bake for 20 min at 350F on a cookie sheet (but use one that isn't non-stick so you can cut the pizza on it).
