sloppy_joes
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people): 4
prep: 15
cook: 40
time:40

1 lb ground beef
1 # onions
300 ml pasta sauce
1 can Campell's Condensed Soup Vegetable Hearty Vegetable with Pasta OR
1 can Ministroni

Heat the ground beef in the microwave until not hard.  Chop the onions.  Fry it.  Fry the onions with it.  Put everything in a pot (or the same frying pan) and bring to a boil.  Simmer 10 min, stirring occasionally.
