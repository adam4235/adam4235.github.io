vegan_sushi
vegetable:2
meat:0
yield (meals * people):3
prep.:30
cook:20
recipe source:Chris

1 # carrot
0.5 # sweet potatoes
1 # avocado
0.5 # red pepper OR
0.5 # green pepper
1.5 c sushi rice
5 # seaweed sheets

-Cook the rice
-Grate the carrots and sweet potatoes, chop the avocado and pepper
-Lay out each seaweed sheet on a sushi mat, put rice on top, put vegetables on top of that, roll it up
