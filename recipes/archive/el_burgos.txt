el_burgos
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):4
prep.:30
cook:60
recipe source:More-with-Less

5 # potatoes
1 lb ground beef
1 # green pepper
1 # onion
2 shredded_c cheddar cheese
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 c tomato sauce

Peel & Slice the potatoes, putting them in a pot
Almost cover with water
Start them cooking
Fry ground beef
Chop green pepper & onion
Add to ground beef once ready
Preheat oven to 350F
Combine other ingredients in a bowl
Put in lasagna dish or casserole dish - alternate beef and potato layers, pouring the cheese-potato mixture on top
Bake for 30 minutes
