hamburger_casserole
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):4
prep.:10
cook:120
recipe source:More-with-less

1 lb ground beef
1 c uncooked rice
1 # carrot
1 # onion
2 cans tomato soup
1 tsp salt
1 bit pepper
2 c water

-Preheat oven to 325F
-Boil water
-Get casserole bowl with cover ready
-Dice carrots & chop onions, putting in bowl
-Put everything in bowl
-Bake for 2 hours
