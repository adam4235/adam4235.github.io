pasta_primavera
vegetable:1
meat:0
yield (meals * people):4
prep.:5
cook:12
recipe source:Pasta Pro instruction booklet

16 oz pasta noodles
2 cloves garlic
1 c carrots
1 c peas
9 oz green beans
1 oz Alfredo pasta sauce
1 tbsp parsely
1.5 tsp basil
0.25 tsp pepper

Bring water to a boil in Pasta Pro.  
Add noodles and vegetables.  
Cook pasta for as long as the package says.  
Drain.  
Place back on heat and immediately add other ingredients.  
Toss lightly and serve.

Note: original recipe called for 0.33 c parmesan cheese
