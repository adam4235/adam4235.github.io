yield (meals * people):3

recipe source:Simply in Season

2 # sweet potatoes
2 stalks celery
1 # red peppers
2 # green onions
1 tbsp parsley
0.25 c apple cider vinegar
2 tbsp oil
1.5 tbsp mustard
1 tbsp maple syrup

Chop the sweet potatoes up small and steam for about 7 minutes or until soft when poked.
Chop the other vegetables, adding to a large bowl.
Add the sweet potatoes and parsley to the bowl, mix together.
Mix the dressing in a separate small bowl.
Add the dressing and mix everything together.
