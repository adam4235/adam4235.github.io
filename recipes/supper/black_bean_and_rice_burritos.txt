vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):4
prep.:30 min
cook:80 min
recipe source: https://gimmedelicious.com/crispy-black-bean-and-rice-burritos/

1 c black beans
1 c cooked rice
0.5 c corn
0.5 # bell pepper
0.5 # onion
0.25 chopped_c cilantro
1 tbsp coconut oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp cumin
0.5 # lime
1 bit cheese
1 bit salsa

Soak the beans overnight (if using dried beans) and cook them in the Instant Pot
Add the coconut oil to a large skillet
Add the corn
Chop and add the pepper
Chop and add the onion
Chop and add the cilantro
Add the rice
Fry everything for about 10-20 min on low.  As it fries, squeeze in the juice from the lime and add the spices
Add cheese and salsa
When making a wrap, can fry it in a frying pan for about a minute on each side to make it crispy, perhaps adding a bit of oil to the frying pan.
