vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):4
prep.:45
cook:15
recipe source:Mom (from a cookbook, but I'm not sure which one)

0.75 c lentils
1 clove garlic
1 c walnut pieces OR
1 c pumpkin seeds
1.5 tsp oregano
1.5 tsp cumin
1.5 tsp chili powder (much less if it's the spicy kind from Sequoia)
0.25 tsp salt
0.25 tsp black pepper
2 tbsp oil
1 tsp maple syrup
2 # green peppers
1 # onion
1 package tortilla wraps
1 jar salsa OR
1 bit nothing
1 # avocado OR
1 bit nothing

Cook the lentils, adding enough water to them (15 min in instant pot)
While they're cooking, chop and fry the green peppers and onion
In a food processor, mince the garlic
Add lentils and walnuts / pumpkin seeds to the food processor, use it to chop them but not fully blend them
Move the mixture to a bowl
Add the spices, oil and syrup, mix
Put the mixture in a wrap with the fried vegetables
You can optionally add other things, like salsa and avocado
