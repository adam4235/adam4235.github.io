leek_potato_soup
vegetable:2
meat:0
yield (meals * people):5
prep.:10
cook:30
recipe source: http://www.cookuk.mobi/soup_starter/leek-potato-soup.htm

3 # leeks
2 tbsp oil
1 clove garlic
2 # potatoes
4 c water
3 # soup cubes
0.25 tsp salt
0.25 tsp black pepper
1 c milk

-Cut up leeks, cutting off the bad part and washing them well
-Heat up the oil in a large pot
-Cut up garlic
-Add the leeks and garlic, fry for 5-10 min
-Cut up potatoes
-Add everything except milk, boil for 15 min
-Add the milk
