moms_vegetable_soup
vegetable:2
meat:0
yield (meals * people):8
prep.:20
cook:30
recipe source:Mom

1 # onion
2 stalks celery
1 cloves garlic
6 c water
3 cubes soup cubes
1 # potatoes
2 # carrots
1 c peas
0.25 c noodles
1 bit salt
1 bit black pepper (mom likes lots of pepper)
0.5 tsp thyme
0.5 tsp parsley
0.25 tsp oregano
0.25 tsp savory

-Chop onion and celery and mince garlic, adding them to the Instant Pot
-Scrub potatoes and carrots
-Chop carrots, adding them to the pot
-Chop potato, adding it to the pot
-Add everything else
-Pressure cook for 15 minutes
