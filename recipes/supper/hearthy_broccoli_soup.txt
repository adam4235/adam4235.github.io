2 # potatoes
1 # onions
2 # carrots
1 stalks celery
1 c water
0.5 bunches broccoli
4 c milk
2 # soup cubes
1 tsp maple syrup
0.5 tsp salt
1 bit pepper
0.33 c flour

Chop potatoes, onion, carrots, and celery, adding them to a pot.
Add water and cook for 5 min.
Chop and add broccoli, cook for 5-10 more min.
Add 3/4 of the milk, soup cubes, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper, heat to boiling.
Whisk 1/4 of the milk and the flour in a bowl.  Stir into the soup until thickened.

Note: original recipe also had cheese.
Note: original recipe had Worcestershire sauce.  I replaced it with maple syrup and more salt.
