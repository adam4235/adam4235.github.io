noodle_salad
vegetable:1
meat:0
yield (meals * people):4
prep.:20 min.
cook:10 min.

2 c noodles
2 # carrots
2 stalks celery
2 c peas
6 spoonfuls salad dressing

Cook the noodles. Let them cool down.
Cook the peas.
Peel the carrot and dice it.
Cut up the celery into small pieces.
Mix everything in a large bowl (can use a different amount of dressing - just go by the appearance).

Note: original recipe had cubes of cheese
