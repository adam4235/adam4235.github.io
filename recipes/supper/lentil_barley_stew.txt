lentil_barley_stew
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):10
prep.:20
cook:70
recipe source:more-with-less

2 stalks celery
1 # onion
6 c water
0.75 c lentils
0.75 c barley
2 tsp salt
0.25 tsp black pepper
0.5 tsp rosemary
1 cloves garlic
1 c tomato sauce
1 # carrot
1 bit mace

-Cut up celery and onion, adding them to the Instant Pot
-In the meantime, dice the garlic and grate the carrots
-Add the rest of the ingredients, pressure cook for 30 min

Note: original recipe said to fry in butter first
