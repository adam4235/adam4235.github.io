lentil_barley_stew
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):10
prep.:20
cook:70
recipe source:more-with-less

4 tbsp oil
4 stalks celery
2 # onion
12 c water
1.5 c lentils
1.5 c barley
4 tsp salt
0.5 tsp black pepper
1 tsp rosemary
2 cloves garlic
2 c tomato sauce
2 # carrot
1 bit mace

-Get a large soup pot ready
-Start heating up oil in the pot
-Cut up celery and onion, adding them to be sauted
-After they've been sauted for 5 min, add water and lentils
-Bring to a boil, simmer 20 min
-In the meantime, dice the garlic
-Add the rest of the ingredients except carrots and tomato sauce, boil, simmer 45-60 min.
-Grate carrots
-Add carrots and tomato sauce, simmer 5 min

Note: original recipe said butter instead of oil
