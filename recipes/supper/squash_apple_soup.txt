squash_apple_soup
vegetable:1
meat:1
yield (meals * people):6
prep.:20
cook:25
recipe source: GI diet cookbook (mom's)

1 # buttercup squash
1 # leeks
1 # onion
1 # carrot
1 chopped_tbsp ginger
1 tbsp thyme
0.5 tsp sage
2 # apples
4.5 c water
4 # soup cubes
1 c navy beans

-Overnight, soak beans, then boil for 2-3 hours (if using dried beans) or 40 min in pressure cooker
-Chop vegetables, adding to pot
-chop apples into small pieces, adding them
-Add everything
-boil for 20 min, stirring occasionally (or 15 min in instant pot)
-Optionally garnish with a bit of chives

Note: original recipe had apple juice instead of one of the apples, and white kidney beans, and said to fry the vegetables in oil
