1 # eggs

Crack the egg & put it in a normal bowl.  Beat it with a fork in a
circular motion (using a beater makes it splash).  Fry it in the
frying pan at medium heat after possibly putting butter in the frying
pan.  Flip quite soon, (when the bottom is cooked).
