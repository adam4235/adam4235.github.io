zucchini_bread
vegetable:0
meat:0
yield (meals * people):8
prep.:20
cook:60
recipe source:More-with-less cookbook

3 c flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
3 tsp cinnamon
3 # eggs
0.75 c sugar
1 c vegetable oil
1 # zucchini
1 tbsp vanilla
1 chopped_c nuts

-Mix the dry ingredients (first 5) in a mixing bowl.
-Beat together the wet ingredients (all except nuts) in a large mixing bowl.  Can use a fork but beat well.
-Preheat oven to 350F
-Add dry ingredients and stir until blended.
-Coarsely chop the nuts.
-Add them.
-Put in bread pans or meatloaf dish.
-Bake for 1 hour.
-Remove and cool on rack. (? - last time it fell apart when I tried to take it out while hot.)
