Recipe source: https://www.mindbodygreen.com/0-12119/a-homemade-vegan-mayo-recipe-that-will-blow-your-mind.html (modified)

0.5 c soy milk
0.5 tsp salt
1 tsp dry mustard
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp apple cider vinegar
2 tsp maple syrup
1 c olive oil

Blend everything except oil in the blender on low.
With the blender running, open the top of the blender and slowly pour in the oil.
Keep blending until it's smooth.
