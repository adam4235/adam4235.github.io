Based on recipes in Simply in Season

3 c rolled oats
1.25 c nuts
1 c flour
0.5 tsp salt
0.25 tsp ground ginger
5 tbsp honey
0.25 c oil
0.2 c water
0.5 c raisins

Preheat oven (ideally toaster oven) to 325F
Mix dry ingredients except raisins in a big bowl
Add liquid ingredients
Stir to mix well
Put in baking pan
Bake for about 35 min, flipping halfway through.  Check once in a while to see if it's browning.
