quick_chocolate_cake
vegetable:0
meat:0
yield (meals * people):8
prep.:20
cook:25
recipe source:Doris

1.5 c unbleached flour
1 c white sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
4 tsp cocoa powder
6 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tbsp vinegar
1 tsp vanilla
1 c water

-get a square 8" x 8" baking pan
-Preheat oven to 375F
-In baking pan stir together the flour, sugar, salt, soda, and cocoa powder.
-With a spoon make 3 wells or holes in the dry ingredients.
-In one hole put the oil, in another the vinegar, and in the other the
vanilla.
-Pour water over it all and stir until thoroughly mixed.
-Bake at 375F for 25 minutes.
