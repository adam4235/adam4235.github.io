1/4 c butter
2.5 c icing sugar
3 tbsp milk
3 tbsp cocoa (if you want chocolate icing) OR
1 bit nothing
1/4 tsp vanilla OR
1 bit nothing

-soften butter (but don't melt) in a big bowl
-Mix dry ingredients in another bowl
-Alternately add dry ingredients and (milk and vanilla) to the butter.  Add about a teaspoon of milk at a time.  Mix with a spoon or fork after each addition.
