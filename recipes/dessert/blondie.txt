blondie
vegetable:0
meat:1
yield (meals * people):2
prep.:5
cook:0
recipe source: http://www.rawfoodrecipes.com/recipes/the-5minute-singleserving-raw-vegan-blondie.html

0.25 c walnuts
0.25 c cashews
2 tbsp sugar
3 # dates
0.125 tsp vanilla extract
1 pinch salt

Blend walnuts, cashews and sugar in food processor
Add a date at a time, blend
Add vanilla extra and salt, blend
Form into a reverse muffin tin shape on a plate
