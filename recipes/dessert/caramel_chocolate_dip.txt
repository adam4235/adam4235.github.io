Here is the Caramel Apple Dip recipe:

1 pkg of cream cheese
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup + 2 Tablespoons sour cream
Carmel sauce
skor bars 3 broken in pieces

Blend first 3 ingred. then spread on pie plate, next spread Carmel sauce over and top with broken skor bar bits.
