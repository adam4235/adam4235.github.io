-You can throw a bunch of old rings from jars in the bottom of a big pot to prevent the bottom from rattling.  This way you don't need a special canner.
-To test if Jam is done: put a plate in the fridge beforehand, then put a bit of jam on the plate and put it in the fridge.  In a few minutes, it should jelly a bit (not be runny) if it's done.
