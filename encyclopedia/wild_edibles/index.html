Eating wild mushrooms:

There are many different varieties, and it's quite dangerous to try mushrooms without being sure. Requires a lot of research, and checking different features (not just sight) - e.g. what it's growing on, what it smells like.

Some beginner types of mushrooms to try:

  • Chanterelle
  • Shaggymane
  • Helecium
  • Oyster
  • Boletus
